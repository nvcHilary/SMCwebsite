Latest news

Christmas Play

The youth of our church will be presenting a play entitled "The True Spirit of Christmas- Giving, Receiving and Believing" from 29th Nov. to 1st Dec. 2012. Tickets are now on sale and can be bought after every mass at the church porch. The play will be held at Peter Pang Hall and ticket prices are as follows: RM10/-, RM20/- and RM50/- each. Please check the bulletin for more details and contact persons.


Year of Faith and Confirmation Mass

Bishop Sebastian Francis officially launched the Year of Faith in St. Michael's church after the Confirmation Mass on 21st Oct 2012. A total of 47 youth were confirmed on this joyous occassion. Photos can be viewed at the archives page.


Parish Pastoral Assembly 2012

The annual Parish Pastoral Assembly will be held on 25th November 2012. Guidelines for submission of questions and suggestions before the Assembly are detailed in the church bulletin. REMINDER:- All Evaluation Forms from Lay Ministries and BECs pertaining to their respective outreach programs for the year are to be submitted by 30th September 2012.


Episcopal Ordination of Bishop Sebastian Francis

Click here to watch excerpts from the episcopal ordination of Bishop Sebastian Francis, new Bishop for our diocese of Penang, at the Church of St. Anne,Bukit Mertajam on 20th August 2012.


Congratulations and Welcome to Fr. Aloysius Tan

The Parish Priests and parishioners warmly welcome Fr. Aloysius Tan to the church of St. Michael. Fr. Aloysius Tan was ordained in the Church of Our Lady of Sorrows,Penang, on the 25th of June 2012. Click here to view pictures from the ordination ceremony. To watch the video of the ceremony,click here for part 1 and here for part 2.


Rite of Christian Initiation for Adults(RCIA)

The new session for RCIA faith journey will begin in June 2012. There will be an orientation on Sunday, 10th June 2012 at 4.00p.m.in St. Theresa's room. If you know of anyone interested in joining the Christian faith, kindly contact the Parish Office for further details. Enrolment forms are also available in church at the back of the pews.
