INQUIRIES

Becoming a Catholic

Adults who may be interested in exploring or joining the Roman Catholic Church will need to undergo the Rite of Christian Initiation of Adults (RCIA). This is a process whereby the inquirer is gradually introduced to the Catholic faith and way of life.

For RCIA inquiries, please contact the parish office.

To find out about our usual mass, rosary and devotional schedules, please click on the "Key Info" link.

Marriage

Couples who intend to marry in the church are to make an appointment to meet with the Parish Priest personally.

To find out about booking rates for the Parish Activity Centre weddings, please click on the "Key Info" link.

Funerals

Please come to the parish office to complete the Notification of Death form and bring along a copy of the deceased's death certificate.

To find out about rental of the Funeral Parlour, please click on the "Key Info" link.

To purchase a niche in the Columbarium, please contact the parish office directly.
