Year of Faith


Throughout the Year of Faith, our parish of St Michael will be having a series of Faith Formation Sessions every month on Friday evenings starting from January 11th, 2013 onwards. Please check with the weekly bulletin to view the times and topics for each month's formation sessions. For Lent, there will be Way of the Cross in place of these sessions.

Click here to read more about the Holy Father's intentions and what we can do to deepen and share our faith in this Year of Faith.



St. Michael's Church Ang Pow Packets


The Evangelization group from the Chinese Apostolate of St Michael's Church,Ipoh are selling ang pow packets with holy pictures and Christian blessings printed on them. There are five different designs and ten pieces per design. One pack containing 50 ang pows is only RM10/=. All proceeds of the sale will go towards the group's evangelization works.Please contact members of the Chinese Apostolate if you wish to purchase these ang pow packets.
