KEY INFORMATION

Click here for our Christmas and New Year mass schedules. For every 1st Friday of the month, there will be Adoration of the Blessed Sacrament followed by Benediction starting at 7.30p.m., followed by Novena to the Sacred Heart and Daily Mass.

The other daily and weekend mass times are as follows:-


Daily Mass (except Tuesdays,Saturdays & certain Fridays*) : 5.30 p.m.
Daily Mass (Tuesday) : 7.00 a.m.
Daily Mass (Saturday) : 7.00 a.m.

Saturday Sunset Mass (English) : 7.00 p.m.
Sunday Morning Mass (English) : 8.00 a.m.
Sunday Morning Mass (Chinese) : 10.00 a.m.
Sunday Evening Mass (English) : 5.30 p.m.

Padre Pio Novena (Every Thursday) : 5.00 p.m.
Rosary in English (Every Saturday) : 6.30 p.m.
Rosary in Chinese (Every Sunday) : 9.30 a.m.
Devotion to the Sacred Heart (1st Friday* of every month) : 7.30 p.m.
Devotion to Blessed Virgin Mary (13th day of every month except weekends) : 7.30 p.m.
