CHURCH OF ST. MICHAEL, IPOH - KEY INFORMATION

The Chinese New Year Mass in Mandarin will be at 10p.m. on 9th February 2013. The 7.00p.m. sunset mass in English will be on as usual on the same day. All other Sunday masses will be as usual on 10th February 2013.

For every 1st Friday of the month, there will be Adoration of the Blessed Sacrament followed by Benediction starting at 7.30p.m., followed by Novena to the Sacred Heart and Daily Mass. However, during Lent season, this Adoration will be replaced by the Way of the Cross followed by mass.

The other daily and weekend mass times are as follows:-


Daily Mass (except Tuesdays,Saturdays & certain Fridays*) : 5.30 p.m.
Daily Mass (Tuesday) : 7.00 a.m.
Daily Mass (Saturday) : 7.00 a.m.

Saturday Sunset Mass (English) : 7.00 p.m.
Sunday Morning Mass (English) : 8.00 a.m.
Sunday Morning Mass (Chinese) : 10.00 a.m.
Sunday Evening Mass (English) : 5.30 p.m.

Padre Pio Novena (Every Thursday) : 5.00 p.m.
Chaplet to St Michael in English (Every 1st Saturday) : 6.30 p.m.
Rosary in English (Every Saturday except 1st Saturday) : 6.30 p.m.
Chaplet to St Michael in Chinese (Every 1st and 3rd Sunday) : 9.30 a.m.
Rosary in Chinese (Every 2nd,4th and 5th Sunday) : 9.30 a.m.
Devotion to the Sacred Heart (1st Friday* of every month) : 7.30 p.m.
Devotion to Blessed Virgin Mary (13th day of every month except weekends) : 7.30 p.m.
