CHURCH OF ST.MICHAEL, IPOH - DIARY OF PAST EVENTS IN 2013

Ponggal - 19th to 20th January 2013

The parish's Indian Group celebrated the Indian Harvest Festival "Ponggal" on Sunday,20th Jan 2013. The traditional boiling of milk in a clay pot was done at the porch of the church before mass. Parishioners were treated to a display of colourful kolam making at the Parish Activity Centre on the 19th of January 2013.

Blood Donation Campaign - 6th January 2013



A blood donation campaign was organised by the Parish Human Development Committe (PHDC) on 6th January 2013 in aid of Fatimah Hospital. A big "Thank You" to all parishioners and volunteers who helped to make this event a success.
